Zenoti gives you access to all your Zenoti data to customize and build features on top of what Zenoti offers. APIs give you access to all your data when you want it, enabling you to create a functionality that is not yet part of the core product experience. This is a win-win situation. While we at Zenoti focus on the core product features, you have the freedom to build custom features.

Updated about 3 years ago

- You can build a customized booking workflow specific to your business needs, than what Zenoti currently offers.
- You can import bookings from external sources, other than Webstore and the customer mobile application.
- You can create appointments from a marketing campaign.
- You can include more fields in your appointments form than what is currently available in Zenoti.

- You can import custom employee block out from external applications into Zenoti for a seamless experience.
- You can include the holiday and leave calendar of employees from your external application and integrate it with Zenoti.
- You can terminate employees or manage employee movement within your organization.
For more information, refer to Employees API.

- You can track leads and guests from an external application into Zenoti.
- You can build custom workflow for loyalty points tracking.
- You can build a custom guest profile.
For more information, refer to Guests API.

- You can build gift cards with a custom amount and template.
- You can create gift cards for your unique custom occasions beyond what Zenoti offers.
- You can sell gift cards beyond Zenoti through other platforms.
For more information, refer to Gift cards API.

- You can freeze and unfreeze memberships directly from APIs
- You can build and sell custom memberships for specific demographics - Analyze zones or centers that have shown interest in some services and you want to build a custom membership plan around it
- You can upsell specific memberships to custom target segments - Let's say customers in Region A are more interested in membership benefits, so anytime a booking comes from this region, you can target region A with a custom membership workflow to increase your revenue
For more information, refer to Memberships API.

- Build custom series packages as per your requirement
- Sell custom packages for specific demographics to increase revenue
- Upsell specific packages to custom target segments
- Create custom refund and redeem model for packages
For more information, refer to Packages API.

- You can book or cancel a series of classes
- You can create custom workshops
- You can create a custom discount flow for payments in fitness
- You can create custom fees for fitness classes
For more information, refer to Classes API.
